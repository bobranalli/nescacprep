Admissions Resources: By Students, For Students

About Us

Nescac Guide is an admissions resource for high school students interested in NESCAC colleges. The site is run by recent NESCAC graduates, all of whom are eager to connect with new applicants. As students who were once in your shoes, we're here to help. We want to see ambitious students get the same opportunities and experiences we were able to have.

Our student team also works closely with NESCAC faculty and college counselors from some of the best boarding schools in the world, like Phillips Academy Andover. That said, NescacGuide.com is not directly affiliated with the official NESCAC organization or any of the schools within it.

We are more than happy to connect you to additional resources, and we are open to any suggestions from students and parents. Keep scrolling for more content that might be of use, or visit our Team Page to meet the core members of Nescac Guide, who can help you on your journey. Lastly, feel free to check out our blogs on the NESCAC schools and their admission process.


The Problem We Solve

We noticed there weren't many online recourses for students interested in the NESCAC schools, and we wanted to change that. Our team members attended these schools, and we know exactly what kind of applicant they look for. We want to be the leaders in NESCAC prep, by providing all the resources we wish we had!
